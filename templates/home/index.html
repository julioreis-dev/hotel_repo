Hotel Resort

Westgate Lakes Resort & Spa in Orlando, Florida is a lakefront, full-service resort with spacious villas, seven pools and hot tubs, a luxury spa, an 18-hole mini golf course, fitness center, basketball, tennis, and volleyball courts, as well as a variety of onsite food and beverage options. The Orlando resort also features the brand-new Treasure Cove Water Park, a 161,000-gallon, pirate-themed aquatic amenity guaranteed to add a splash to your stay. The resort is centrally located near your favorite Orlando theme parks and attractions, and when you return, you can relax by the pool or in your villa, your home away from home.
